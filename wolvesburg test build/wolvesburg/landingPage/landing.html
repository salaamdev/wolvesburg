Thank You For Downloading WolvesBurg!

WolvesBurg is a chromium extension that enhances your experience in wolvesville, a multiplayer game of social deduction. With WolvesBurg, you can automate various tasks such as spinning the gold and rose wheels, earning gold in the background, identifying individual messages in the chat, and playing the game for you in custom modes to gain experience points. And there is more to come in the future.

WolvesBurg is an independent extension created and maintained by RedScorpion that has no affiliation with wolvesville or its developers.
